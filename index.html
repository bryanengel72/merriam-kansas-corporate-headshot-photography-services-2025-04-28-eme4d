Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear for my corporate headshot?

We recommend solid colors and professional attire that matches your industry. For corporate roles, traditional business attire works best - suits, blazers, and conservative colors. We'll provide a detailed styling guide upon booking.

How long does a headshot session take?

Individual sessions typically last 45-60 minutes, allowing time for multiple poses, expressions, and outfit changes. Team sessions are scheduled based on group size, with approximately 15-20 minutes per person.

What's included in the session fee?

Our standard session includes professional lighting setup, expert direction, multiple poses and expressions, basic retouching, and 3 final high-resolution digital images with full usage rights.

How quickly will I receive my photos?

You'll receive a gallery of proofs within 24 hours of your session. After selecting your preferred images, final retouched photos are delivered within 48 hours via digital download.

Do you offer hair and makeup services?

Yes, we partner with professional makeup artists and hair stylists who specialize in camera-ready looks. These services can be added to any session for an additional fee.
